Business Insider published U.S. Regional Economic Map – November 2013.

This map is extremely informative for those of us with normal color vision. However, Figure 2 simulates what people with one form of color vision deficiency, deuteranopes, will see.
Deuteranope simulation of Business Insider's economic map 

The simulation uses www.vischeck.com. It is very easy to check our color schemes with a color vision simulator and to choose colors that everyone can see.
